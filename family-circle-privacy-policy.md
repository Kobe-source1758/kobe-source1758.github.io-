# Privacy Policy for Family Circle

**Effective Date:** 2025-05-09

## 1. Information We Collect

Family Circle collects the following information:
- **Email address** - Used for account authentication via Google Sign-In
- **Profile information** - Your name and profile picture (if you choose to provide them)
- **Connection data** - Information about people you add as connections or invite to the app

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide the app's core functionality:
- Creating and managing your account
- Adding and organizing your personal connections
- Sending and receiving invitations to connect with others
- Displaying your profile to people you've invited

## 3. Data Storage

All data is securely stored in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). Firebase uses industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit.

## 4. Your Privacy Choices

- **Connections you add** are visible only to you - they are your private notes
- **Invited connections** can see your profile after accepting an invitation
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time

## 5. Third-Party Services

This app uses:
- **Firebase Authentication** - For secure login
- **Cloud Firestore** - For data storage
- **Google Sign-In** - For authentication

These services may collect additional information as described in their respective privacy policies.
